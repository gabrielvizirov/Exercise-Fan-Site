What is the game about?

Marvel vs Capcom Infinite is a fighting game, created by Capcom, where characters from the Marvel Universe battle against the Capcom Universe. It is a 2v2 game where the player can switch places with his/her partner and continue combos to defeat the oponent. This is the first time the series has a story mode.


How to play:

This is a 4 button game where there are 2 punch and 2 kick bottons. You press the buttons is a certain combination to create combos. Each character has special moves that are done pressing down to forward, or down to back, is a sweeping motion. These special moves can connect with regular attacks also to make combos. There is a button to switch characters and there is a button to use the infinity gems. Infinity gems are player chosen moves that give bonuses to the player such as faster attack, shooting homing fireballs, and much more. The goal of the game is to deplete the other players health bar while attacking them.
